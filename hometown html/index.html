Ukraine

About

Ukraine is a country located in eastern Europe. The capital is Kyiv, located on the Dnipro River. It is a magnicefent independent country, every part of each has fascinating nature, kind, hard-working and brave people, unique and authentic culture. Its history is abudant with fights for independency, for the right to sing Ukranian songs, to write, read, publish Ukranian poems, books wriiten, to speak Ukranian, to be free and happy. Its history about pationate and courageous people who loved, love and will love their land, who are happy to call theselves Ukranians.

National Anthem

Original lyrics

Ще не вмерла України
Ні слава, ні воляю
Ще нам, браття українці,
Усміхнеться доля.

Згинуть наші вороженьки,
Як роса на сонці,
Запануєм і ми, браття,
У своїй сторонці,

Душу й тіло ми положим
За нашу свободу,
І покажем, що ми, браття,
Козацького роду.

Translation

Ukraine's glory has not yet died,
Nor her freedom,
Our destiny, brothers Ukranians,
Shall yet smile to us.

Our enemies shall die
Like the dew under the sun.
And we, as well, shall
Dominate in our homeland.

Soul and body we will
Give up for our freedom,
And show that we, brothers,
Are from the Cossack kin.

Culture

Ukranian culture is very versatile. It's a big country and every region has their own traditions and customs. However, even though we can be different we stay united, unseperable. If you want to understand Ukranians and our culture, you should try reading our books, poems, listen to our songs, come and see our nature, architecture, art, try our national cuisine, read about our history.
